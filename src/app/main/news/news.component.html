Annual Club Championship Tournament 2024

March 15, 2024 Tournament News

We are excited to announce the upcoming Annual Club Championship Tournament 2024, marking another year of exceptional golf and friendly competition at our prestigious club.

This year's championship promises to be our most exciting yet, featuring both seasoned players and emerging talents from our membership. The tournament will take place over two weekends, allowing participants to showcase their skills across our challenging course layout.

The championship will feature multiple divisions to ensure competitive matches across all skill levels. Our ground staff has been working tirelessly to prepare the course to championship standards, with particular attention paid to the greens and hazards that make our course unique.
