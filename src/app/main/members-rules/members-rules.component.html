Members Rules

General Club Rules

1. Dress Code

  • Appropriate golf attire is required at all times on the course and practice areas
  • Collared shirts or mock turtlenecks are mandatory for men
  • No denim, t-shirts, or athletic shorts permitted
  • Golf shoes with soft spikes or flat-soled shoes only

2. Tee Time Policies

  • Members may book tee times up to 14 days in advance
  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to tee time
  • Members must register in the Pro Shop before playing
  • Guest policies and fees apply as per membership category

3. Course Etiquette

  • Repair all ball marks and divots
  • Rake bunkers after use
  • Maintain pace of play (4 hours per round maximum)
  • Follow cart path rules and daily usage guidelines

4. Practice Facility Rules

  • Practice areas open from sunrise to sunset
  • Use designated practice balls only
  • Follow safety guidelines and designated hitting areas
  • Junior members must be supervised by an adult

Important Notes

• The club reserves the right to modify rules and policies as needed

• Violation of club rules may result in disciplinary action

• Members are responsible for their guests' adherence to club rules

• Special events may have modified rules and requirements
